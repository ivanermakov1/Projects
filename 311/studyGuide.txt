Heap and Priority Queue

Heapify : O(n)

Sift Down / Sift Up: O(log n)

Insertion: O(log n)

Deletion: O(log n)

Extract Max / Extract Min: O(log n)

Get Max / Get Min: O(1)

Parent: (i - 1)/2

Left Child: 2 * i + 1

Right Child: 2 * i + 2

Last Internal Node: size/2 -1

First Leaf: size/2 

i < size/2 = internal nodes

i >= size/2 = leaves
